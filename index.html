Groovy Sales Agent App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

Enhaz Beverages Pvt Ltd ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company"), operating under the brand name Groovy, is committed to protecting the privacy of its employees and sales agents. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected through the Groovy Sales Agent mobile application (the "App").

This App is an internal workforce management tool intended exclusively for authorized employees and field sales agents of Enhaz Beverages Pvt Ltd. It is not intended for public or consumer use.

By accessing or using the App as part of your employment, you acknowledge and consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data, which is directly linked to the user’s identity, solely to provide the App’s required business functionality:

  • Account and Login Information: User credentials created and managed by authorized company administrators. The App does not allow self-registration.
  • Precise Location Data: GPS location coordinates collected approximately every 30 seconds while the user is logged in and clocked in.
  • Usage and Activity Data: Clock-in and clock-out timestamps, sales entries (such as visits, orders, and notes), and limited technical information including device identifiers and IP address.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, health data, contacts, or personal messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Track employee attendance and work hours.
  • Verify field presence, movement, and routes.
  • Generate internal sales and performance reports.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies and operational needs.

Personal data is used strictly for internal business operations and workforce management purposes.

3. Location Data Usage Disclosure

The Groovy Sales Agent App collects precise location data as a core functional requirement.

  • Location data is collected only while the user is actively logged in and clocked in during work hours.
  • Location data may be collected in both foreground and background modes to ensure accurate field activity verification.
  • Location tracking automatically stops when the user clocks out or logs out of the App.
  • The App does not track location outside of work hours or during off-duty periods.

Location data is never used for advertising, marketing, or personal tracking.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data or share it with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

  • Authorized company administrators and managers.
  • Trusted service providers (such as AWS) for secure hosting, subject to strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect company rights and assets.

We do not use personal data for cross-app tracking, behavioral profiling, or third-party analytics unrelated to App functionality.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure storage on AWS infrastructure with access controls.
  • Industry-standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to meet operational, contractual, and legal obligations. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to or correction of your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, including historical location data, subject to legal and employment requirements.

Requests are typically processed within 30 days.

8. Consent and Permissions

Use of this App forms part of your employment responsibilities. Where required by the operating system, the App will request permission before accessing location services.

Denying required permissions may limit or prevent the App from functioning correctly for work-related purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
